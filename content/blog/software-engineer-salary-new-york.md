## How New York Compares to Global Tech Hubs

While New York-specific figures are not yet available in our dataset, software engineer compensation in comparable major cities gives useful context. In London, mid-level engineers earn a median of £85,000 and senior engineers a median of £125,000 (GBP, source: Levels.fyi 2024). In Dublin, mid-level engineers earn a median of €78,000 and senior engineers €110,000. In Amsterdam, senior engineers earn a median of €108,000. In Berlin, mid-level engineers earn a median of €72,000. In Paris, mid-level engineers earn a median of €62,000 and senior engineers €90,000. In Barcelona, mid-level engineers earn a median of €52,000. These figures are provided in local currencies and are not directly comparable to USD compensation in New York without currency and cost-of-living adjustments.

## Software Engineer Salaries by Seniority: Switzerland Benchmark

Switzerland offers one of the highest software engineer pay scales globally and serves as a useful upper-bound benchmark. Nationally, junior engineers earn a median of CHF 88,000 (range: CHF 75,000–100,000), mid-level engineers earn a median of CHF 113,000 (range: CHF 95,000–132,000), and senior engineers earn a median of CHF 150,000 (range: CHF 130,000–178,000). In Zurich specifically, figures are higher: junior median CHF 96,000, mid-level median CHF 125,000, and senior median CHF 165,000 (range: CHF 142,000–195,000). Source: Swiss Federal Statistical Office LSE 2022.

## What Drives Software Engineer Pay in Major Cities

Compensation for software engineers varies significantly based on seniority, industry sector, company size, and local labor market conditions. In high-cost metros like New York, total compensation packages often include base salary, equity, and annual bonuses — meaning base salary alone may understate true earnings. Seniority level is consistently the strongest predictor of pay across all markets represented in our data, with senior engineers earning roughly 1.5x to 2x the median of junior engineers in the same location.

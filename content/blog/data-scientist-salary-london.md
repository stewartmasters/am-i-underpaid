London is one of Europe's most active markets for data science talent. This page breaks down current data scientist salary benchmarks for London using 2024 compensation data, so you can assess where your pay stands relative to the market.

## London Data Scientist Salary: Key Figures

For a mid-level data scientist in London, 2024 benchmark data shows the following salary range in GBP: minimum £65,000, median £82,000, and maximum £105,000. The median figure of £82,000 is the most reliable reference point for professionals at the mid-seniority level. The spread between the minimum and maximum reflects variation across industries, company sizes, and specific technical skill sets. Source: Levels.fyi (2024).

## What Counts as a Competitive Salary at Mid Level?

Based on available data, a mid-level data scientist earning at or above £82,000 in London is at the median for their seniority band. Salaries below £65,000 may indicate underpayment relative to the current market, while those approaching or exceeding £105,000 reflect the upper end of the mid-level range — typically seen at well-funded tech firms or in specialist domains such as machine learning infrastructure or NLP.

## Factors That Influence Pay in London

Several variables push individual salaries above or below the median. Industry sector plays a significant role — finance, fintech, and large technology companies typically pay above the median, while public sector and early-stage startups may pay below it. Specialisations in deep learning, causal inference, or MLOps often command a premium. Years of experience within the mid-level band, the size of the employer, and whether total compensation includes equity or bonuses also affect the effective package.

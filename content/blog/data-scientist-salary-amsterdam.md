## Amsterdam Data Scientist Salary: Key Figures

Based on 2024 compensation data for mid-level data scientists in Amsterdam, the salary range runs from €55,000 at the lower end to €92,000 at the upper end, with a median of €72,000 per year. All figures are in EUR and reflect gross annual base salary. These benchmarks are sourced from levels.fyi and represent mid-seniority roles. Confidence on this dataset is currently moderate, as record volume is limited — treat these figures as a directional guide rather than a definitive market rate.

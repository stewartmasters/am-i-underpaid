If you are a DevOps Engineer based in Berlin or considering a move there, understanding the local compensation landscape is essential for negotiating fairly. This page breaks down 2024 salary benchmarks for mid-level DevOps Engineers in Berlin, based on available market data.

## Berlin DevOps Engineer Salary: Key Figures

For a mid-level DevOps Engineer in Berlin, the 2024 benchmark data shows the following gross annual salary range in EUR: a floor of 60,000 EUR, a median of 74,000 EUR, and a ceiling of 92,000 EUR. These figures represent the broad spread of compensation across the Berlin market and serve as a useful baseline when evaluating an offer or preparing for a salary review. The median of 74,000 EUR is the most reliable single reference point for a mid-level professional in this role.

## What the Salary Range Tells You

The gap between the minimum (60,000 EUR) and maximum (92,000 EUR) is approximately 32,000 EUR, which reflects meaningful variation in how Berlin employers compensate DevOps Engineers. Factors driving this spread typically include company size, industry sector, specific tooling expertise, and individual negotiation. Engineers at the lower end of the range are often newer to the mid-level tier or working at smaller companies, while those at the upper end tend to hold specialised skills or work at well-funded tech firms.

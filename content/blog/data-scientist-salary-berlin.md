## Berlin Data Scientist Salary: Key Figures

For a mid-level data scientist in Berlin, 2024 compensation data shows the following range in EUR: salary minimum of 58,000, median of 72,000, and a maximum of 90,000. The median of 72,000 EUR is the most useful reference point — it represents the midpoint of the market for professionals at this seniority level. If your current salary falls below 58,000 EUR, you may be meaningfully underpaid relative to Berlin market norms for a mid-level role. Source: Levels.fyi (2024). Note: confidence rating for this dataset is 0.45, indicating a moderate sample size. Treat these figures as directional benchmarks rather than definitive market rates.

Even within a single city and seniority band, individual salaries vary considerably.

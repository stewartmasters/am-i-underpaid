## Switzerland as a High-End Benchmark

Switzerland, and Zurich in particular, represents the upper end of software engineer compensation in the data available. These figures are in CHF and reflect a high cost-of-living market.

Nationally (CHF): Junior engineers earn a median of 88,000 (range: 75,000 to 100,000). Mid-level engineers earn a median of 113,000 (range: 95,000 to 132,000). Senior engineers earn a median of 150,000 (range: 130,000 to 178,000).

Zurich specifically (CHF): Junior engineers earn a median of 96,000 (range: 82,000 to 110,000). Mid-level engineers earn a median of 125,000 (range: 105,000 to 146,000). Senior engineers earn a median of 165,000 (range: 142,000 to 195,000).

Source: Swiss Federal Statistical Office Labour Structure Survey (LSE-2022-CH), confidence rating 0.47. These figures are not directly comparable to CAD-denominated Toronto salaries without currency and cost-of-living adjustments.

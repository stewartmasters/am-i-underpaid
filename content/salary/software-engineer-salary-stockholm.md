Software engineers in Stockholm earn more than the Swedish national average across all seniority levels. This page breaks down salary ranges for junior, mid-level, and senior engineers in Stockholm, using data from the SCB Salary and Employment Survey (2022). Figures are in EUR.

## Stockholm Salary Overview by Seniority

The table below summarises the salary ranges for software engineers in Stockholm across three seniority levels. All figures are in EUR and sourced from SCB-SES-2022-SE.

Junior: Min EUR 41,000 — Median EUR 48,000 — Max EUR 57,000
Mid-level: Min EUR 54,000 — Median EUR 64,000 — Max EUR 76,000
Senior: Min EUR 72,000 — Median EUR 88,000 — Max EUR 106,000

The median senior salary of EUR 88,000 is notably 83% higher than the junior median of EUR 48,000, reflecting the significant premium Stockholm employers place on experience.

## Junior Software Engineer Salary in Stockholm

Junior software engineers in Stockholm — typically those with up to two years of professional experience — can expect a median salary of EUR 48,000 per year. The range runs from EUR 41,000 at the lower end to EUR 57,000 at the upper end. Compared to the Sweden-wide junior median of EUR 43,000, Stockholm-based juniors earn approximately EUR 5,000 more at the median, a premium consistent with the city's higher cost of living and concentration of tech employers.

## Mid-Level Software Engineer Salary in Stockholm

Mid-level engineers in Stockholm — generally those with two to six years of experience — have a median annual salary of EUR 64,000. The salary band stretches from EUR 54,000 to EUR 76,000. The national mid-level median for Sweden sits at EUR 57,000, meaning Stockholm engineers at this level earn around EUR 7,000 more at the median. The wider salary band at this level also reflects greater variation in specialisation, tech stack, and employer type.

## Senior Software Engineer Salary in Stockholm

Senior software engineers command the highest salaries and the widest ranges. In Stockholm, the median senior salary is EUR 88,000, with a range of EUR 72,000 to EUR 106,000. The national senior median is EUR 78,000, placing Stockholm's premium at EUR 10,000 at the median. The upper bound of EUR 106,000 reflects compensation at larger tech firms, scale-ups, and financial technology companies headquartered in the city.

## Stockholm vs. Sweden National Averages

Across all three seniority levels, Stockholm salaries exceed the Sweden-wide benchmarks. The city premium grows with seniority: EUR 5,000 at junior level, EUR 7,000 at mid-level, and EUR 10,000 at senior level. This pattern suggests that as engineers gain experience, Stockholm's competitive talent market rewards them at an increasing rate relative to the national average. Data not available for other Swedish cities for direct comparison.

## Data Notes and Confidence

All salary figures on this page are sourced from the SCB Salary and Employment Survey, data version SCB-SES-2022-SE. The confidence score for all records is 0.45, indicating moderate confidence. This reflects the fact that city-level breakdowns from official statistics surveys carry inherent sampling limitations. Figures should be used as directional benchmarks rather than precise guarantees. Data not available for specialisation-level breakdowns (e.g., backend, frontend, DevOps), company size, or industry sector within Stockholm.

## What Factors Influence ML Engineer Pay in London

Several variables typically drive compensation differences for ML engineers, regardless of location. Years of experience is consistently one of the strongest predictors, with junior, mid-level, and senior roles commanding meaningfully different pay bands. Specialisation also matters: engineers with deep expertise in areas such as large language models, computer vision, or MLOps infrastructure tend to attract higher offers. The hiring sector plays a role too — financial services, adtech, and well-funded AI startups in London often benchmark against global talent markets, which can push salaries above the city average. Company size and funding stage are additional factors, as pre-IPO and Series B-plus companies frequently use equity to supplement base pay.

## Key Factors That Influence Software Engineer Pay

Regardless of location, several factors consistently drive compensation differences for software engineers. Seniority and years of experience are the strongest predictors of base salary. Specialisation in areas such as machine learning, cloud infrastructure, or security typically commands a premium over generalist roles. Company size and funding stage also matter — larger enterprises and well-funded scale-ups tend to pay above market median. Finally, total compensation packages often include equity, bonuses, and benefits that are not captured in base salary figures alone.

Software engineers in Dublin earn some of the highest salaries in Europe, driven by the city's dense concentration of multinational tech employers. This page breaks down current salary benchmarks by seniority level using data from Levels.fyi and Eurostat SES, so you can see exactly where your compensation stands.

## Dublin Software Engineer Salary at a Glance

Across seniority levels, Dublin software engineer salaries range from €40,000 at the junior end to €140,000 at the top of the senior band. The figures below are drawn from two sources: Levels.fyi (2024) for Dublin-specific mid and senior data, and Eurostat Structure of Earnings Survey (SES-2022) for broader Ireland and Dublin benchmarks. Confidence scores for these datasets range from 0.31 to 0.45, meaning figures should be treated as indicative benchmarks rather than precise market rates. All figures are gross annual base salary in EUR.

## Junior Software Engineer Salary in Dublin

Dublin-specific junior salary data is not available in the current dataset. For Ireland nationally, Eurostat SES (2022) data shows junior software engineers earning between €40,000 and €58,000, with a median of €48,000. Dublin salaries for junior roles typically sit above the national figure given the city's higher cost of living and concentration of large tech employers, but a precise Dublin-specific range is not available for this seniority level.

## Mid-Level Software Engineer Salary in Dublin

For mid-level software engineers, two Dublin-specific data points are available. Levels.fyi (2024) places the range at €60,000 to €100,000, with a median of €78,000. Eurostat SES (2022) data for Dublin shows a tighter range of €52,000 to €82,000, with a median of €65,000. The gap between these sources likely reflects differences in the companies surveyed — Levels.fyi skews toward larger tech firms, while Eurostat SES captures a broader cross-section of employers. Mid-level engineers at major tech multinationals should reference the upper end of these ranges.

## Senior Software Engineer Salary in Dublin

Senior software engineers command the widest salary range in Dublin. Levels.fyi (2024) data shows a range of €90,000 to €140,000, with a median of €110,000. For comparison, Eurostat SES (2022) data for Ireland nationally puts the senior range at €72,000 to €115,000, with a median of €90,000. The Levels.fyi Dublin figures are notably higher, consistent with that source's focus on large technology companies headquartered or with major offices in the city. Data not available for Dublin-specific senior figures from Eurostat SES.

## What Drives Software Engineer Pay in Dublin

Dublin's software engineering market is heavily shaped by the European headquarters of major US technology companies, which tend to pay at the top of the market. Beyond base salary, total compensation at these firms often includes equity, bonuses, and benefits — but this page covers base salary only, as total compensation data is not available for this dataset. Factors that influence where an individual falls within these ranges include years of experience, technical specialisation, company size, and negotiation. Data not available for compensation breakdowns by tech stack or company tier within Dublin.

## Key Salary Figures for Product Managers in France

Based on Eurostat Structure of Earnings Survey data (SES-2022-FR), mid-level product managers in France earn the following: Minimum: EUR 45,000 per year. Median: EUR 58,000 per year. Maximum: EUR 74,000 per year. These figures represent gross annual compensation. Paris, as the primary economic hub in France, typically sits at or above the national median, though city-specific figures are not available in the current dataset.

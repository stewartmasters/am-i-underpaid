## Finance Analyst Salaries in Switzerland

Switzerland represents the highest compensation band in our current European dataset for finance analysts. Junior analysts earn between CHF 72,000 and CHF 104,000, with a median of CHF 86,000. Mid-level analysts see a median of CHF 116,000, ranging from CHF 96,000 to CHF 140,000. Senior analysts reach a median of CHF 158,000, with a range of CHF 132,000 to CHF 190,000. Data source: BFS Labour Structure Survey (LSE-2022-CH). Confidence score: 0.47.

## Finance Analyst Salaries in Sweden

Sweden sits in the mid-to-upper tier for European finance analyst pay. Junior analysts earn a median of EUR 42,000 (range: EUR 34,000 to EUR 51,000). Mid-level analysts have a median of EUR 55,000, ranging from EUR 45,000 to EUR 66,000. Senior analysts earn a median of EUR 72,000, with a range of EUR 59,000 to EUR 88,000. Data source: SCB Structure of Earnings Survey (SCB-SES-2022-SE). Confidence score: 0.45.

## Finance Analyst Salaries in Italy and Poland

Italy and Poland represent the lower-to-mid range of European finance analyst compensation. In Italy, junior analysts earn a median of EUR 27,000 (range: EUR 22,000 to EUR 34,000), mid-level analysts earn a median of EUR 36,000 (range: EUR 28,000 to EUR 46,000), and senior analysts earn a median of EUR 50,000 (range: EUR 38,000 to EUR 65,000). Data source: ISTAT Structure of Earnings Survey (ISTAT-SES-2022-IT). In Poland, junior analysts earn a median of EUR 18,000 (range: EUR 13,000 to EUR 24,000), mid-level analysts earn a median of EUR 27,000 (range: EUR 20,000 to EUR 37,000), and senior analysts earn a median of EUR 43,000 (range: EUR 30,000 to EUR 59,000). Data source: GUS Earnings Structure Survey (GUS-BSW-2022-PL). Confidence scores: 0.44 and 0.43 respectively.

## Finance Analyst Salaries in Portugal

Portugal sits at the lower end of the European compensation spectrum for this role. Junior finance analysts earn a median of EUR 18,000 (range: EUR 13,000 to EUR 24,000). Mid-level analysts earn a median of EUR 25,000, ranging from EUR 19,000 to EUR 33,000. Senior analysts earn a median of EUR 36,000, with a range of EUR 26,000 to EUR 48,000. Data source: INE Portugal Earnings Survey (INE-PT-IEG-2022-PT). Confidence score: 0.44.

DevOps Engineers in London are among the better-compensated technical professionals in the UK market. This page breaks down current salary ranges by seniority level, using 2024 compensation data, to help you benchmark your pay accurately.

## Salary Overview: Mid-Level DevOps Engineer in London

For mid-level DevOps Engineers in London, the 2024 salary range runs from £65,000 at the lower end to £105,000 at the upper end, with a median of £82,000. If you are sitting below £65,000 in this role, it is worth investigating whether your compensation reflects the current market. The median of £82,000 represents a reasonable benchmark for professionals with a few years of hands-on experience in CI/CD pipelines, infrastructure-as-code, and cloud platforms.

## Salary Overview: Senior DevOps Engineer in London

Senior DevOps Engineers in London command significantly higher compensation. The 2024 data shows a range of £95,000 to £150,000, with a median of £118,000. At this level, professionals are typically expected to lead infrastructure strategy, mentor junior engineers, and own reliability and scalability decisions. The gap between mid and senior median salaries — £82,000 versus £118,000 — represents a £36,000 uplift, underlining the strong premium London employers place on seniority and ownership.

Experience with Kubernetes, Terraform, and observability tooling is increasingly expected at senior level. Employer type also matters: financial services and large tech firms typically pay above the median, while early-stage startups may offer equity in place of top-of-band cash. ## How to Use This Data in Salary Negotiations

These figures are a starting point, not a ceiling.
